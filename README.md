About Involute Sans

Involute Sans is the official brand typeface of Involute Tech, Bangladesh — 
meticulously crafted to ensure clarity, consistency, and professionalism in all English 
brand communications. It reflects Involute’s modern, innovative, and 
forward-thinking identity.
With its semi-condensed letterforms, Involute Sans offers a harmonious blend of 
warmth and precision. Its strong typographic structure conveys both stability and 
innovation, making it highly versatile for use across digital interfaces, corporate 
materials, and printed publications.
This custom-designed sans-serif typeface is exclusively developed for Involute and 
comes in three essential styles: Regular, Italic, and Bold.
This font guideline provides a complete reference for designers and users to apply 
the Involute Sans font family correctly and consistently across all brand touchpoints.
